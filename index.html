Instructions

Concentration, known as Match Match, Memory Game, Pelmanism, Shinkel, Suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs is a card game in which all of cards are all face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
